# Operations

The curated entry point to how the company runs day to day — processes, runbooks, policies, and rituals.

## Purpose

Hold the durable "how we operate" knowledge so that recurring work is consistent and does not depend on any one person's memory.

## Context

This is `10 Operations`, part of the Execution layer in the [[Repository Blueprint]]. Operations documents are typically `guide`-type runbooks and processes (use `01 Kernel/Templates/Guide.md`). They connect to the [[Business Map]] (strategy) and the [[Decision Map]] (the decisions that shaped them).
