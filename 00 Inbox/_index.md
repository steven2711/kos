# Inbox

The capture layer. Raw, unprocessed input lands here and waits to be modeled and promoted into canonical knowledge.

## Purpose

Give every fleeting idea, voice note, scratch thought, and meeting dump a single landing zone, so nothing is lost and nothing canonical is written from unprocessed capture (Constitution, Article VIII).

## Context

This is `00 Inbox`, Layer 0 in the [[Repository Blueprint]]. **Nothing here is canonical.** Items leave the Inbox by being modeled and linked per the [[Knowledge Modeling Guide]], then promoted into the correct folder once they pass the [[Quality Gates]].

## What belongs here

- Voice notes and their transcripts.
- Scratch thoughts and half-formed ideas.
- Raw meeting captures (before extraction into [[Meeting|meeting]] notes, [[ADR|adr]] decisions, and [[Question|question]] documents).
- Anything you want to keep but have not yet placed.

## How to process the Inbox

1. Pick an item.
2. Classify its `type` (see [[Frontmatter Specification]]).
3. Search for an existing canonical home (see [[AI Contributor Guide]]).
4. Model and link it; create or extend the right document.
5. Move it to its canonical folder, or archive it. The Inbox should trend toward empty.
