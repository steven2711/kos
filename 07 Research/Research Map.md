# Research Map

The curated entry point to open investigations, experiments, and first-class open questions.

## Purpose

Keep every unknown visible and tracked. Questions are first-class objects; they must never disappear into prose or meeting notes (Constitution, Article VI).

## Context

This map curates `07 Research` (see [[Repository Blueprint]]). A [[Question|question]] evolves into [[Research|research]], an [[Experiment|experiment]], or a decision in the [[Decision Map]]. Findings that become durable knowledge are promoted to concepts in the [[Domain Map]]. Maps curate, they do not auto-dump (see [[Linking Standards]]).
